From Subculture To Mainstream: Examining Animes Transformation From Microculture Into Global Mainstream Through User Engagements

The anime culture has become a global phenomenon that significantly influences popular culture and shapes the entertainment industry worldwide with skyrocketed revenues and unprecedented international growth in recent years.

This research examines the key factors of animes global market expansion through anime content creations and distributions and analyzes their implications for stakeholders and investors as well as regular anime fans.

  • Improving Anime Fan Experience
  • Current Obstacles With Anime Market
  • Improving Industry Stakeholders Experience
